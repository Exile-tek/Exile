                            LIMITED USE ITEMS
							
 Some items have limits on there use on the server, this is done to
 prevent griefing, world damage or server instability.

Ender-Thermic Pump - Limit of 1 per person and only in resource world
 or the nether

Block breaker - Limit of 6 per person

Mob farms - Limit of 6 per mob type per person

IC2 Miner - Limited to resource world

Quarry - Limited to resource world

 There may also be limits on what rank you have to be to use a item,
 Please check the player rank screen or website for more details
